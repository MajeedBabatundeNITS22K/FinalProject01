History of Kokkola

Kokkola, located in Finland, is a city with a rich history dating back to the 17th century. The city is known for its beautiful old wooden buildings and maritime heritage. Over the centuries, Kokkola has grown into a modern city with a vibrant culture and economy.

General Weather Information

Kokkola experiences a typical Finnish climate with mild summers and cold winters. Summer temperatures range from 15°C to 25°C, while winter temperatures can drop below freezing. Snowfall is common during winter months, and the city offers various winter sports and activities.
